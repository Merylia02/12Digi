About This Web


This is a website about recycling (specificaly plastic) and what happens when you don’t properly dispose. This website will talk about what is happening in our world and what are the consequences of our actions. This website will also tell you how you and make a change to help improve the state of our world.


Context


What is happening:

  • People wasting resources
  • People not properly recycling
  • Pollution in our ocean and land

The consequences:

  • Plastic kills animals
  • Plastic destroyes our eco system
  • Plastic ends up in our bodies

What to do:

  • Properly Recycle
  • Buy Plastic free products
  • Pick up rubbish you see that isn’t treated properly
